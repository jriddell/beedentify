Bee & Wasp Identification

Bumble Bees

There are many varieties of bumblebees, with different coloured markings and of varying sizes. Unfortunately, we are unable to assist with removing bumblebees or their nests. However, bumblebees are generally very quiet and not usually aggressive, only build small nests, and are not usually a problem to people or animals. In almost all cases they can be left alone, and will not cause you any issues. For more information, please read about bumble bees on the Bumblebee Conservation Trust website.
